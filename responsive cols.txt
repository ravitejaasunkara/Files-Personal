this means in large screens it will take 3 out of 12 columns so 3*4 four divs can be displayed in one row, similarly, in medium screens, it will take 4/12 that means 
4*3 three divs can be displayed in a row whereas in small screens it will take 6/12 columns, meaning 6*2 two divs can be displayed in a row.
